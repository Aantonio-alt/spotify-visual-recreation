YOASOBI is a band that creates “music from novels”. The band members are Ayase (composer) and ikura (vocals). Their first single “Into The Night” released on November 2019 has been streamed more than 700 million times on December 2021 and ranked No.1 on Billboard Japan Total song chart “HOT 100”. YOASOBI also ranked No.1 on Spotify’s “most streamed Japanese artist in the overseas market in the year 2021”. The song “Monster” was chosen for ”The 10 Best Songs of 2021” on the U.S. “TIME” magazine, and YOASOBI continues to draw international attention. YOASOBI continues to expand the scope of their development beyond music, including the publication of original novels and turning them into movies.
